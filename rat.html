Year of the Rat

Though people consider the rat as a pest, and it even makes its way into derogatory language, it ranks first in the Chinese zodiac. Its characteristics are of an animal with spirit, wit, alertness, delicacy, flexibility and vitality. Some lucky signs for the rat are the numbers 2 and 3, the colours gold, blue and violet, flowers like lilies and african violets, and the directions southeast and northeast.

Personality of the Rat

People born in the Year of the Rat are instinctive, acute and alert in nature which make them to be brilliant businessmen. They can always react properly before the worst circumstances take place, and are also sophisticated and popular in social interactions. They are sanguine and very adaptable, being popular with others. Their strengths are their adaptability, intellect and out-going personality, while their weaknesses are their stubborn, picky and timid nature.
